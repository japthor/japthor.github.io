City Cleaner 3D

Hyper-Casual Game where you jump right into the dirty work and start scrubbing to make the city sparkle again like the good old days.

  • Engine: Unity/C#.
  • Company: Kwalee.
  • Status: Completed.
  • Download: iOS.

The game includes several mini-games, such as using a tow truck to clear the road of parked cars or throw away the trash piling up on the sidewalks. I was involved in creating one of the mini-games and solving bugs/issues.
