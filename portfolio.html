Some Projects

Check out some of the projects where I have been involved.

A Dose Of You

Game Project - Unity/C#

New game from Super Mega Team and still in WIP! The demo was playable at Valencia Indie Summit

Some of my tasks were: Creating the Map, scene events, pairs minigame, find objects UI, the dialogues and bug tracking. It is included the integration of both art and animations.

Hopefully, there will be more advances in the future!

Fearless: A Brand New Tale

Game Project - Unreal/C++

A third-person platform game made by four programmers and four artists, all students from the last year in ESAT.

I was in charge of programming the UI, some mechanics such as climbing or simulating using a parachute, integrating animations with their state machine, saving the game, the pickups and incorporating music and fx sounds.

Music as Gameplay

Game Project - Unity/C#

A game where the music is the protagonist. The objective of the player is to avoid certain elements of the scene that are totally manipulated by some information that a simple background music can provide us such as, for example, frequency.

Some audio processing techniques including Fast Fourier Transform has been used.

Passmen City

Simulation Project - Unity/C#

A simulator game made by four programmers where you can see the interaction between various types of agents, such as pedestrians or cars, in a procedurally generated city.

Includes: Procedural city generation, Decision trees for agents (FSM), Crowd/Flocking system (pedestrians), Real-time pathfinding (cars and pedestrians) and Neural Networks (pedestrian learning).

Pastorse-Engine

Graphics Engine - C++/Opengl

My own personal engine version. It was created from scratch!

The project includes the following elements: Creation of Geometry (Cube), Different lights (Spotlights, Directional Light and Point Light), Shadows, Add textures, PostProcess, Nodes, Integrate created Models... and more.

Jump360
